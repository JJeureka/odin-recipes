Description

This is a simple yet elegant recipe for some bomb ass asparagus that will improve your life immensely. The grill makes even large asparagus tender and leaves it with a lovely smokey flavor

Ingredients

  • Asparagus
  • Olive oil
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp sea salt

Steps

  1. Preheat grill
  2. Mix oil, asparagus, and spices in large bowl
  3. Place asparagus on grill, perpendicular to the grill rods
  4. Cook until asparagus is soft
  5. ENJOY!
