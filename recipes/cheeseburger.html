Cheeseburger

Description

This is a bomb ass cheeseburger that will improve your life immensely. No cap. Nothing fancy, but it gets the job done.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef or turkey
  • sliced cheese of your choosing
  • 1 tsp of cayenne pepper
  • 1 tbsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • burger buns

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill
  2. Combine meat and spices in a large bowl
  3. Mix up and form into patties
  4. Put on grill and cook, flipping as needed
  5. Add buns to grill right before burgers are done
  6. Add cheese to burgers and allow cheese to melt
  7. put cheeseburgers in buns
  8. EAT!
