Chile

Description

This is some bomb ass chile that will improve your life immensely. It is a bit spicy and surprisingly sweet with the shredded carrots. I know what you're thinking, wtf buts shredded carrots in their chile. Believe me, it works!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef or turkey
  • 1 16 oz can of tomato sauce
  • 1 8 oz can of tomato paste
  • half an onion, chopped
  • 1 jalapeno, chopped
  • 1/2 cup of carrots, finely shredded
  • A wee bit of oil
  • 1 16oz can of pinto beans
  • 1 16oz can of kidney beans
  • 1 tsp of cayenne pepper
  • 1 tbsp chile powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. heat oil in large pan
  2. saute onion and jalapeno
  3. Add and cook ground meat
  4. Add tomato paste and tomato sauce
  5. Add beans
  6. Add carrots, finely shredded
  7. Add spices
  8. Simmer for several hours until carrots are dissolved
